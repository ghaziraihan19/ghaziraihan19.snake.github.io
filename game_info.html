Snake Game History

Snake is a classic game that was first introduced in the late 1970s. The game became very popular during the era of vintage mobile phones, especially on Nokia devices.

How to Play

1. Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to control the snake.

2. Eat the food scattered on the screen to make your snake grow longer.

3. Avoid hitting the walls or your snake's own tail. If that happens, the game will end.

4. Try to achieve the highest score!
